    Head of Social Media - The New School Free Press

    I began as a Social Media Editor for the New School Free Press in January 2024 and have since advanced to be Head of Socials. As Head of Socials, I create posts, schedule content and manage a team of social media editors to ensure that the online presence of NSFP is aligned with our visual and journalistic standards.

    The New York City subway remains largely inaccessible to the disabled community

    I worked on this project about subway accessibility during my first semester on the Free Press. I wrote this story and created the visuals.

    Freecap - A Weekly Recap

    One of my first projects as Head of Social Media was the creation of 'freecap' --a weekly recap of the Free Press on our Instagram Story. The templates were designed in collaboration with NSFP Creative Director Ana Whelley, but the logo and maintenance of the series are done by me. These are posted every Sunday!
