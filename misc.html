    Miscellaneous Design Projects

    Here's a collection of some of my favorite design projects that are disconnected from any of my bigger projects!

    Book Cover Design - The Rainbow Letters

    In late 2021 I designed the book cover for The Rainbow Letters: The Temerity To Believe. This was great experience for my design career, as it was my first time working with a client who had a specific vision.

    Design Project - Mercurys Mainstage

    For my Design Systems for Brands Class, I designed a branding system for a music festival based on the existing design system of my favorite Seattle coffee place Mercurys Coffee Co. This is not a perfect brand manual, but I spent so long on it that I refuse not to put it on my website! Click on the photo to explore the full PDF.

    Marketing Intern - Seams Better

    In the summer of 2024 I interned for the sustainable fashion company Seams Better. I've highlighted some of my favorite social media design work I did with them; but I also learned how to communicate with partner brands and maintain an email newsletter!

    Design Project - Hurricane Posters

    For one of my first design classes at Parsons, I designed 3 different posters for Bob Dylan's song 'Hurricane.' The first uses only type and letterforms, the second uses type and limited graphic elements and the third uses type and image. In all honesty this project was grueling, but I'm very happy with the final products!
